2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide to us directly and information that is automatically collected when you use the Service.

A. Personal Data

When you use our Service, we may ask you to provide certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:

  • Email address (for account creation, order delivery, and notifications).

B. User Content (Images)

To provide our AI clothing swap service, you must upload images ("User Content").

  • Original Photos: Photos of yourself or models.
  • Garment Images: Photos of the clothes you wish to try on.

How we handle your images:

  • Processing: Your images are uploaded to our secure servers solely for the purpose of processing the AI generation.
  • Retention: We value your privacy. Original photos and generated results are stored temporarily to allow you to download them. We automatically delete your uploaded images and generated results from our servers within 24 hours.
  • No Training: We do not use your personally identifiable uploaded photos to train our public AI models without your explicit consent.
